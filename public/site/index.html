About Our Church

A lighthouse for our city — shining hope, love & purpose.

Southampton Lighthouse International Church is a Christ-centered family drawn from more than twenty nations. We gather to worship together, grow in faith, and serve our city with practical love. Everyone is welcome — from those exploring faith for the first time to those who've walked with Jesus for decades.

Our Mission

To reach people with the love of Christ and disciple them into a lifelong walk of faith.

Our Vision

A thriving, diverse community that shines the light of Jesus into every corner of Southampton.

Our Values

Worship, family, generosity, integrity, and a deep love for the Word of God.

Our Ministries

There's a place for every member of your family.

From little ones to grandparents — we've built spaces where you can grow, connect, and serve.

Children

A safe, joyful space where children (0–11) discover the love of Jesus through play, worship and creative storytelling.

Youth

Teenagers (12–18) gather weekly for real conversations, mentorship, worship and life-changing friendships.

Families

Marriage, parenting and family life support — including small groups and seasonal family celebrations.

Worship

Our worship team leads us into God's presence with heartfelt, contemporary and multi-lingual songs of praise.

Prayer

Weekly prayer meetings, prayer chains, and personal prayer support — because prayer changes everything.

Outreach

Feeding programs, refugee support, community events and mission partnerships across Southampton and beyond.

What to expect on Sunday.

Whether it's your first time in a church or you've been coming for years, we've made it simple to feel at home from the moment you walk in.

  • Location: Kingsland Square, St Mary Street, Southampton, SO14 1NF
  • Parking: Free on-street parking available on Sundays, plus nearby public car parks
  • Accessibility: Step-free access, accessible restrooms and hearing loop system
  • Dress code: Come as you are — smart, casual, or anything in between
